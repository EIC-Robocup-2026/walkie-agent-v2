For the Receptionist challenge, treat every new face as a **guest to enrol**:
capture the face embedding and store the guest's **name** and **favourite drink**
together so they can be recalled and re-introduced later. When you recognise a
returning guest, surface their stored name and drink instead of asking again.
Be precise about *which* guest you're reporting on — the main agent relies on you
to keep the identities straight.
