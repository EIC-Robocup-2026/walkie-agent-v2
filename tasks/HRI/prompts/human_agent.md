For the HRI (receptionist) challenge, treat every arriving guest as a **person to
enrol**: capture the face embedding and store the guest's **name** and **favourite
drink** together, so they can be recalled and re-introduced even after guests
switch seats. When you recognise a returning/seated guest, surface their stored
name and drink instead of asking again. Be precise about *which* guest you're
reporting — the main agent relies on you to keep the two identities straight.
